Features

Here Are Some Features Of My Web Pages

My Home page has some informations of Sweet Life such as our Hisory, a Menu and three Stores location.

Every page has a same menu that allow users to navigate through other pages. My menu position is fixed, meaning it stays fixed at the top of the viewport even when scrolling, it has links to other pages to be more friendly with user. I also added transition effects for color and box shadow when hover.

In Register page, i create a form for user to regisrer, i used javascript to validate, to check if all inputs are valid or not. Some effects for the register form and a background color to decorate. Click to see Register page 

Next is the Order page where people can order their flavourite ice cream. In this page you can see the menu our store with some effects when hover. You can order by click on the product that you want, a order form will pop up, there are two payment methods for you to pay. I used javascript to make sure when the checkbox with label “same as delivery address” is checked, the fields for billing address will be filled with the delivery address automatically. If the delivery address is not completed when this checkbox is checked, an alert: “Please enter your delivery address first” will be displayed. I also allow users to choose from three types of credit card: Visa, MasterCard and American Express and limit the length of the credit card number, 16 digits for Visa and MasterCard, 15 digits for American Express. Click to see Order page 
