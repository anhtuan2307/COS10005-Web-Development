Menu Animation: When hovering over menu items (a tags) within the .menu, the text color changes to #7B3B3B, and a text shadow effect is applied with a transition duration of 0.3s.

Welcome Text Animation: The welcome text inside the .text class has a transition effect applied to the .letter class, which moves the letters upward when hovered over. Additionally, there is a shadow effect applied to the letters (text-shadow) with a transition duration of 5s. Click to view!!!

Images Animations: Images with the class .anhhanoi, .hobbies, and .anhgioithieu have a transition effect applied to their outline and background color.

Form Input Animations: The submit button within the form has a box-shadow effect applied with a transition duration of 0.1s. When hovered over, the box-shadow changes to 0 5px 10px black.
